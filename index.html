Highlights

Here are some of the main projects I've worked on recently.

Iridescent

A poetic puzzle game about loss, memory and acceptance. You will have to change your point of view to move on, in more ways than one.

Escapastamp (In Progress)

Escapastamp is a goofy coop summer camp simulator, where you have to collect hidden stamps on an island full of secrets.

Uncannycal Girls

Uncannycal Girls is a two player shoot'em up game where you play as magical girls with a dark secret.

Smaller Projects

Cesame

Cesame is a program that displays graphs and metrics about a computer's hardware and performance.

Guitar Invaders

Guitar Invaders is an alternative input game where you control a spaceship with a real electric guitar.

Sonic Snails

Sonic Snails is a 2D snail racing platformer.
